🎬   Panoramic World Generation   🎬

A framework leveraging image and video diffusion models for direct 8K panoramic world generation in two minutes, without requiring optimization. Relevant projects include: 4K4DGen (ICLR Spotlight) and InstructLayout (T-PAMI 25).

🎬   Explicit 3D World Generation   🎬

A unified framework leveraging a video diffusion transformer directly generates dynamic 3D scenes in seconds, without requiring optimization. Examples include: Diff4Splat (Under Review), MoVieS (Under Review), and DynamicVerse (NeurIPS'25).

📸    Misc: 3D Assets   📸

Additional research focuses on the generation of photorealistic 3D assets from a single image for immersive experiences. Relevant projects include: HumanSplat (NeurIPS 24), Human3R (NeurIPS 25), DiffSplat (ICLR 25), and PartCrafter (NeurIPS 25).
