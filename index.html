Panwang Pan | 潘攀望

I am currently employed as a Senior Researcher at PICO within ByteDance Ltd. Previously, I held the position of Senior Algorithm Engineer at Alibaba Cloud, where I specialized in 3D Reconstruction and 6DoF Pose Estimation.

In 2019, I earned my Master's degree from Xiamen University, where I was enrolled in the School of Informatics.

I focused on generative models and multi-modal representation learning, particularly in the 3D realm. Research contributions have been integrated into XR devices, Aliyun Cloud AI-Box, and various commercial products.

I welcome opportunities for coffee chats and collaborations. Please feel free to reach out if you're interested in my projects.
